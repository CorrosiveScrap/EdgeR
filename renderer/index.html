About

Break free from the shackles of bloat that are attached to all these Edgeware-like programs with a custom commercial build. Unleash your creativity and paint your unique style in every corner of the app, not just the content inside. Elevate your brand and stand out against the crowd.

What we offer

Custom branding

We will do a professional redesign of all cosmetic features from the ground up to match with your unique style.

Exclusion

Pick and choose what you want included. Axe the ability to add files, chuck out custom captions, remove close buttons you control what does and doesn't end up in the final cut.

Unlimited copies

There is no limit on how many people that can use your build. Be it 10, 100, or even 5000 users you will never hit a cap on how many instances you can ship out.

Who's in control?

Once your are happy with the product you will receive a compiled build along with the source code. You are free to change or modify the project in any way after that.

Technical support

If something is broken or not preforming up to standard swift and reliable support is just a message away. Doesn't matter how long its been since the purchase date we will always get an issue fixed.

Pricing
